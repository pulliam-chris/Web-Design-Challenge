Temperature Plot

As can be seen from the scatterplot there is a clear trend between latitude and the expected high temperature for the city. The date information was collected is the Winter season in the northern hemisphere (greater than 0 degrees latitude) and the Summer season in the southern hemisphere (less than 0 degrees latitude). Temperatures trend significantly colder moving away from the equator in the northern hemisphere.
